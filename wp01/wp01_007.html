Edward Mattera: Yes, that's my sister Frannie on the right. The person next to my mother is her sister, (my Aunt Rose). The picture on the deck is from 1976. We went from New York to St. Thomas, Bermuda and St. Martin (When we were in Bermuda one of the stores had a beautiful Waterford Chandelier. It was very expensive. Vicki said "When my ship comes in I'll buy it for you" I said "Your ship DID come in" We both cracked up!!
