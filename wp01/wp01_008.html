Appeared in ”The Thirteen Clocks." a 1950 fantasy novelette that was released in 1976 as a stage play as Duke of Castle Coffin, an unusual baddie who's frozen the castle's clocks as a way to imprison the young Princess Saralinda. A handsome Prince comes to rescue her, with the help of the oddball elfin Golux, played by the elfin Janice Faulkner. I was suitably and ferociously evil, and the Golux was the children's favorite.
