Notes for George Fredrick Coons
Lydia: husband George Fredrick Coons, died 2004. Flew B25s in air force. wanted to grad from MIT, couldn't get grades. Dental School Tufts on Air Force credits. They lived together in East Boston with her parents while in school. Lived in Japan for a while.

Alison Coons: George graduated from high school at age 15. He was accepted at MIT at age 16, but found it boring and slacked off. His father made him withdraw and re-pay him for the year’s tuition, so he needed work. Before he left, he was part of a group of MIT students rounded up to attend a church social. He met Lidia there. He was 16, she was 18. Almost immediately she brought him home to her parents. They took him in, he found work, and eventually worked with Lydia’s cousin Antonio (Nino) on the Mystic Bridge. During WWII he became a pilot. After the war he trained in dentistry at Tufts Dental.
Notes for George Fredrick & Lydia (Family)
Alison Coons: Well, my father went to MIT when he was 16, dropped out-"too boring". He met my mother at a church dance, after that he practically lived at her house (no funny business). He worked with Ni until he turned 18 then he signed up for the army airforce and was in a B-Mitchell bomber cadet class of 43, so he wasn't in Boston much. My mother went to Salem State while that was going on.

When the war ended he went back to East Boston. Three years later he married my mother.

He then asked the airforce to pay for Tufts Dental School (he didn't have and undergraduate degree) Airforce said ok so he went to dental school and my mom worked. I was born in 1950 and when he finished school he was sent to Japan. My mom, grandmom moved to Cape Cod.

When he came back he was briefly stationed near Boston and then when I was 4 he got stationed here in Alb. So I guess we moved away in 1954. But summers we spent a couple of weeks with my auntie Pia ans Uncle John on Harve St. I spent all that time mostly with Brigida. Another couple of weeks we spent in the Adirondacks at my paternal GF's cabin.

We moved about every 3-4 years. My last summer was age 16 after that I finished (1972) university I did go back at age 19 for my Nana's memorial on the way to England where my father was stationed for 4 yrs. After that I was working for the gov along with my ex. So most of our vacations were spent roaming around England, though in 1973 we went all over and spent 2 days in Ischia and about a week in Naples and Rome. Last time I was in East Boston in 91 or 92 for about 10 days. Wish I could go back!.
