Notes for Michele Francesco Mattera
Michele Francesco (Michael) was the second child of Giosue and Nicola. He was born in 1882 in Calimera-Serrara, a village on the southern end of Ischia that today is part of the Serrara-Fontana commune.
Michael married in 1907 in Ischia. Michael emmigrated to the US in 1907, before his first child Nicola was born in 1909.
We don’t know how Michael decided to come to Swampscott. We can speculate the expansion of the town as a kind of summer retreat for the well-to-do created opportunity for construction labor. Michael worked in construction, and eventually started his own construction contracting business.
On the 1930 Census form, Michael is listed as the owner of “658 Humphrey St” valued at $12000. He is listed living at 658b with his wife Lucy and his children “Nichalas, Palmone, Mary, Laura, Joseph, Teresa.” The tenants living at 658a were Tony Carriera, mother Rose, and three brothers.
Michael remained in Swampscott the rest of his life. He died in 1949 at age 66.
