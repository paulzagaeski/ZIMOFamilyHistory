Notes for Vita Grace Giardina
Vita Grace was 12 years younger than her husband Louie.

Frances Saccente: “My father worked in the garment industry, with my Aunt Rose’s (Giardina) husband. He brought my father to meet my mother.

My mother had to quit school, because there were seven children in the family when my grandfather Giardina died of blood poisoning in 1919 (she was 12). She liked to sew. She and my grandmother sewed the pillows for my grandfather’s coffin. She regretted that my father didn’t allow her to go to sewing school.”35
