Notes for Angelo Martin Saccente
Angelo is the oldest son of Frank Saccente and Frances Mattera.
He grew up in the Jamaica section of Queens, NY near his Mattera grandparents store.
Angelo graduated from high school and worked in the trucking industry.

Angelo was married to Donna Blaszyk and had a daughter Julie.
He later married Deborah Grogan, and they have three children, Amanda, Angela, and Michelle. They live in Seldin NY.
Angelo was injured on the job in August 2013 and went on disability.

Angelo is kind of a stereotypical Italian guy from New York. He has strong opinions, sees through people who are all talk, and loves to argue and tease.
Angelo is described by all his cousins as the most generous person they know. He is everyone’s “big brother,” a natural caretaker. He has always stepped in to take charge when the family needed someone to make decisions or take action.
As a boy, Angelo was the guy who made bullies sorry they ever tried to bully one of the Saccente boys.
When Angelo’s sister Lina died, Angelo took in her son John and did all he could to help him recover from his mother’s sudden death.

Angelo made his first trip to Italy in summer 2016 to visit his father’s family in Barre. He stayed a month and found it a wonderful experience.
