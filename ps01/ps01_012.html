Notes for Susan Marie Zagaeski
Susan Marie is the fifth child (fourth surviving) of Ted and Vicki.
Sue was born in Marblehead’s Mary Alley Hospital, was premature, and remained in the hospital for about two months after birth. Fortunately she was otherwise a healthy baby.
As the youngest child and only girl in the family, Sue inevitably had a different childhood experience than her brothers. She attended public school in Marblehead. She had a few close childhood friends who were neighbors. Sue graduated from Marblehead High School in 1980.
Sue had several jobs after high school. She trained as a dental technician, and worked for many years for Dr Angelo Boncore in Marblehead. She also became a Letter Sorting Machine operator for the US Postal Service, and has worked in the Reading MA postal facility for many years.
Sue married Kevin Carroll of Salem in 1988. The marriage was short, and they divorced in 1991. Their son Keegan was born in 1990.
Sue and Kevin bought a house on Varney St in Salem in 1989. When Kevin left, Sue took on responsibility of maintaining and repairing the house. Sue allowed Ted to start a few major renovation projects in the house. The projects were mostly successful, but the delays and interruptions completing them were stressful.
Sue eventually became her mother’s primary caretaker. Vicki was a devoted grandparent to Keegan through his early childhood, caring for him in her Marblehead home while Sue worked. When Vicki sold the Marblehead house and moved to an apartment, she continued to care for Keegan. When Vicki’s health began to fail in 1996, Sue had Vicki move into her Salem home. She had to manage a job, a child, and an ill parent on her own. It was a difficult time.
Sue has remained in her Salem house, has raised Keegan to adulthood on her own, and has been financially independent. She has friendships with co-workers, and has gone on vacation trips with them.
Sue has kept her kindness and her good humor through difficult times. She is a very loyal friend and will never give up on someone who needs help.
