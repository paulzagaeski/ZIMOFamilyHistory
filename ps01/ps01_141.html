Notes for Maria Mattera
Vicki Mattera:
“The third daughter of Zio Stefano is Maria, 51. She is the one who had the alcoholic husband who died. She as two sons, Donato, 28, who is working in Germany as a waiter and Stefano, 26, who works as a waiter in a hotel on Ischia in the summer and is looking for a winter job. Also two daughters, Ida (pronounced eda) 31 who we visited only briefly and Teresa, 23, married with one child. I have forgotten her husband’s name, but he was very nice. Stefano has a girlfriend who helped cook pranzo as did Stefano. When we arrived, he was making fruit salad and she was rolling home made manicotti.

“Maria and Stefano live in the home in Serrara where your father and mine grew up. She has no pictures of her father and I promised to have a copy of the one I sent you made for her. Being there brought back many memories of stories my father told us. Oh how I wish they were both alive anow so I could hear them over again, now that I have been in it. I wish I could have had more time with just Felicia, Nicolina, and Maria to hear more of their early history.”
