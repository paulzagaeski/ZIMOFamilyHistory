Notes for Michele Iacono
Alison Coons: My GF Michele's parents were Francescone Iacono and Elizabeth (unknown last name-probably Iacono). He had a half-sister Mirianinna. No info on her. He studied in the seminary, learned 5 or 6 languages, decided he did not want to be a priest and married Elise (first wife in Ischia). She died in childbirth, later he married my gm. My great grandfather made Michele sell Italian wine in Marsailles after he quit the seminary. Then the double wedding party, immigrated in 1913. He had a printing business (in Boston) and sponsored many relatives into US. My GF died young, my mother was 10.

Lydia Iacono: His first wife, from Serarra, died in childbirth with twins. He was to go into the seminary, but didn't want to become priest. He met Guiseppina, but her father wouldn't give a dowry. Married same day Harry and Gulia, double wedding. Emmigrated same time.
Notes for Michele & Giuseppina (Family)
Michele Iacono and Giuseppina Iacono were married in a double wedding with Giuseppina’s step-sister Gulietta and Enrico Mattera.
