Notes for Frances Jean Mattera
Frances Jean was the third child of Luigi Mattera and Vita Giardina. She was known as Frannie. She was born in Queens NY and grew up there with her older sisters and brothers.
Frances met Frank Saccente and they married in 1955, when she was 22 years old.

2015-01-03 11:36 PM—Ann Cupolo
Aunt Frances didn’t marry again after Frank died. She enjoyed many years with Junior (I don’t know his real name – I hadn’t ever met him). He died last year so Aunt Frances recently moved in with Angelo and his family.

Angelo Saccente 5 Feb 2016:
Frances had a boyfriend, Henry. She met him after husband Frank died in 1964, they were together about five years. Frannie needed help with crazy kids. Henry moved in and he lived with the family 1967-1969.
He tried to be like a drill sergeant, scheduled everything. 
He was a disbarred IRS agent, fired for embezzling.



After Frank died, Frannie tried to run catering business. She soon sold it, went back to teaching. North Babylon school district.
In the 1970’s Frances wrote to President Carter’s mother about Peace Corps. Packed up everything and left for OK. Set up shelter for abused women. Traveled to London and other places about Peace Corps. Worked until about five years ago, 2010.

Frannie traveled to Ischia to visit Matteras in the 1970s. One letter from Gennerino Migliaccio referred to her as “Frenzi.”

Wed 27 Dec 2017 — For the past year, Frannie lived in assisted living, after a year-plus living with Angelo. She remained active and engaged with family, really enjoyed the community of the facility. In summer 2017, her health began to decline—infection, kidney failure, detached retina. In December 2017 she fell and broke her hip. While in recovery after hip replacement, she suffered heart failure from which she didn’t recover.
Medical notes for Frances Jean Mattera
2016-01-10 According to Angelo Saccente:
Frances was diagnosed with congestive heart failure years ago. She kept this information from her kids, while she was living in Oklahoma. After JR died, his daughters were not willing to care for her. After she returned to New York, her leg swelling got worse. Had difficulty walking up from basement apartment. Became sedentary, had a poor diet.

July 2015 suffered a fall. Was hospitalized, went to rehab, returned home, immediately fell again.
Since then, Frances has remained at the St James Nursing Center, St James NY.

Dec 2015 Had Christmas visit with family at son Louie’s home. Contracted bronchitis.
