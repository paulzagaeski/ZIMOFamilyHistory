Notes for Nicolina Mattera
Nicola was the second child of Stefano Mattera. We believe she was born around 1910. We believe she was born in Calimera-Serrara, one of the villages in the area known today as Serrara-Fontana commune at the southern end of Ischia. Her name could be Nicoletta or Nicolina.
Frances Mattera:
“Stefano did get married eventually and had a daughter Nicolina (name of Louis,Stefano's,Enrico's mother). Nicolina marries Generino who is the son of the brother of the bride [who killed herself]. Talk about Romeo & Juilet!!! Generino was here a few times. Nicolina did not like it here. She missed her father (Stefano) and he did die while she was here. She returned to Italy and when I went there in 1977, she was living near the house where my father was born.”
Joe Mattera:
“She had a daughter Rina, and one other child.”
Ed Mattera:
“I visited Generino and Nicola in Ischia, but Generino was in the hospital, too ill to see me. His daughter Rina suffered from some kind of impairment or disability, and she was very unkind and unhelpful. While I was visiting other places in Italy that trip, I tried to call Generino’s house to ask how he was, and she hung up on me.”
