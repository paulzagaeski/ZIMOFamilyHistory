Notes for Enrico Agostino Mattera Jr
Enrico (Harry) was the fourth child of Enrico and Giulia. He was born 12 years after his sister Vicki, when his mother was 38.
We know very little about Harry. He was called Junior at home. He attended public schools and graduated from Swampscott High School in 1947.
Harry attended Brown University in Rhode Island, but was expelled after a run-in with a professor there. Later, Harry Jr applied to Massachusetts College of Pharmacy, but there’s no evidence he attended that school.
Family members told conflicting stories about Harry’s emotional state. There’s general agreement he had manic episodes beginning in his teens. No one talked openly about how the family dealt with this.
In 1954, at age 24, he experienced a severe depression. He went to the basement and committed suicide with a .22 calibre rifle.
Harry Sr, for unknown reasons, kept the weapon after his son killed himself with it.
Medical notes for Enrico Agostino Mattera Jr
Harry Jr suffered mental health issues from adolescence until his death by suicide at age 24. At the time it was described as schizophrenia but modern diagnosis would likely have been depression and bipolar disorder.
