Notes for Giosue Anthony Mattera
Born in Queens. Worked for years in bank operations and security in New York area. Married Barbara, two children. Divorced. Met Catherine while working at a NY bank. When Catherine needed to move back to Atlanta, joined her there.


Recent:
Substitute elemetary school teacher
Volunteer music teacher at Atlanta public schools

Hobbies: opera, recorder, dulcimer.
Fan of jazz and blues. Very knowledgeable about early 1900s jazz artists and 1940s-1960s artists. Dizzie Gillespie, Miles Davis.
In NY, joined the “background” company of Metropolitan Opera. Appeared as background cast in large crowd scenes. Vicki had a photo of Joe in 1700’s era costume and powdered wig.
In 2000s began following artists who perform sea shanty style of music. Calls it “the blues of the sea.” In June 2017, traveled to NY and CT to attend the Mystic Seaport Sea Shanty Festival with daugher Joy.
Avid reader. Fan of mystery fiction. Favorite authors include Michael Connelly (Heronimous Bosch series) and Italian author Andrea Camilleri (Inspector Salvo Montalbano series).

Wed 27 Dec 2017 — Talked to Joe on the passing of his sister Frannie. Joe visited her in NY last summer. He’s suffering from a virus and feels too ill to travel to NY for funeral. Hopes there will be a memorial later.
