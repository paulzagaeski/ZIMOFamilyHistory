Notes for Frank Saccente
Angelo Saccente wrt Frank Saccente birth date (March 2017 text message):
My grandparents left Italy in the 1920s and  in America they had 2 children. 1923 my Aunt Lina, 1925 my dad. They also had my Uncle Angelo  in Italy in 1919. But he never came with them. My cousin told me they wanted to have kids in America to make it easy for them to go back and forth.

Joe Mattera thinks Frank died of a respiratory ailment.
Frank had a nice voice. Sang opera in amateur productions.
Frank’s family is from the central mountains in Italy, village called Barre.

According to Angelo Saccente:
Frank died in 1964, the day after Angelo’s birthday, after suffering a stroke. Survived stroke but couldn’t talk. Died April 15. He was a caterer. Came to US 1949 after ban on immigration from Italy was dropped.
Frank had an older brother, who they knew as Uncle Angelo. Fought in Italian air force, shot down over Russia during WWII.
