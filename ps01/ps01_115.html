Notes for Michał & Anna (Family)
Ted Zagaeski believed there were many children besides his father Walter and his uncle Francis. The number was between seven and eleven. He believed their other uncles and aunts were lost in the Bolshevik Revolution or in World War I or ll, or sent to Siberian workcamps during the Stalin Era in the 1930s.

Ted does not know his grandfather's or grandmother's names.
