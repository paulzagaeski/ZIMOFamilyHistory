Introduction

Hello,

Researching and writing a family history is never finished. Families never stop changing.

Here you’ll find information I’ve collected for the four main lines of my family:

+ Zagaeski (AKA Zagajewski, from Ukraine)
+ Iacono (Ischia, Italy)
+ Mattera (Ischia, Italy)
+ Olszewski (also from Ukraine)
That makes the acronym ZIMO.

It’s a work in progress. There are mistakes and missing information. I hope you’ll connect with me to correct and add information for our future generations.

I’ve researched some historical information to find out more about past generations. I’ve also collected stories from family members. Some of those stories (the ones fit for public reading!) are in the Notes in each Person Sheet or Family Card in the site. Be sure to click on each person to see if we have a story about them yet!

It would be wonderful to have more media files: documents, photos, audio recordings, and video. But most of all, your stories and your memories of your own life and those around you are the most valuable parts of this project. This is a resource for every family member, and I hope it will continue to grow.

My contact info is here. Get in touch!

If you’re ready to share, here’s the list of basic information you can send:
• Full Name (middle, maiden, and so on)
• Dates of birth and death, plus places for both
• Spouse name, and their dates
• Children’s names, and their dates
• The place where the person spent most of their life
• What they did for work, and what was their education
• Any significant fact or story about them and their life

PLEASE NOTE: This is a private website. Unlike Ancestry.com or other genealogy services, this info is not on the Internet. It sits on my laptop. Only someone who has been given the web address by me or someone I trust can see the site. That means the information here can’t be found by an Internet search. Personal information that appears here is not being shared publicly. I also have a policy of restricting information—things told to me in confidence do not appear on the site. This is a hobby for me, and I only share this information with those in the family who are interested.

TO NAVIGATE IN THE SITE:
-The default view is a Family Card—it shows a person, their spouse, and their children. You click on names or icons in the Family Card to open windows and navigate through a family.
-Click on a person’s name to open their Person Sheet (facts and stories about one person and about their spouse and children)
-Click on a person’s parent name to open that parent’s Family Card
-Click on a person’s child to open that child’s Family Card
-Anywhere you see a Camera icon, you’ll find a page with pictures. Be sure to click the Camera next to a couple’s Marriage date to find Family pictures.
-Click Back to go back to the current Family Card.
-When you click a Tree icon, you’ll see the ancestors of the current person. You can navigate through a family in the Tree view by clicking names or by using dropdowns to select children.
-Click a footnote number to see a source document.
-If you get lost, click Contents to come back to this page.
-Use your browser’s Zoom to make pictures larger.
